Section 3
03What We Collect & Why
Subscription Validation

Used to manage and update your subscription status. We collect:

  • In-App purchase receipts (validated with Apple)
  • Subscription status and entitlement checks
  • Subscription product fetch requests
Usage Analytics

App usage analytics are minimal but essential to help us run and improve our products. We collect:

  • Application starts and ends
  • Device locale (language/region)
  • Device type, CPU architecture, and OS version

We use Firebase Analytics to collect and manage this data. All data is anonymized and aggregated.

Crash Logs

Crash logs are automatically sent on the next App start. These logs contain anonymized data about the crash to help us identify causes and provide fixes. They do not contain any of your personal or financial data. We collect:

  • Anonymized bundle identifier and short bundle version string
  • Device type, CPU architecture, and OS version
  • Timestamp when the crash occurred
  • A generated UUID to prevent duplicate reports
  • Plain-text exception class name and message (if applicable)
  • Low-level crash data: instruction pointer, method/function names, signal data
  • The application log file

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect and manage this data.

Data You Provide Directly
  • Your name and display preferences (stored locally only)
  • Email address — only when you contact us for support
  • Financial data you enter (expenses, income, categories, budgets, notes) — stored locally on your device only
  • Profile preferences (avatar, currency, goals, theme) — stored locally only
